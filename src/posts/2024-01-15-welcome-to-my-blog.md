Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm excited to launch this blog where I'll be sharing my thoughts and experiences across the many areas I'm passionate about.

## What You Can Expect

This blog will cover a wide range of topics that reflect my diverse interests and professional experiences:

### Technology & Cybersecurity
With over 20 years in IT and my current role as Deputy IT Director and CISO, I'll share insights on:
- Cybersecurity best practices for small organizations
- IT infrastructure and management
- Emerging threats and how to defend against them
- Technology solutions for rural communities

### Emergency Management
As someone deeply involved in emergency management and ARES, I'll discuss:
- Emergency preparedness for individuals and communities
- Ham radio and emergency communications
- Lessons learned from real-world incidents
- Building resilient communities

### Personal Projects
I love tinkering with technology in my spare time, so expect posts about:
- DIY IoT projects for home and emergency use
- Electronics projects and experiments
- Home automation and security systems

### Life in Small-Town Idaho
Living in Genesee, Idaho has given me a unique perspective on:
- The challenges and benefits of rural technology infrastructure
- Community building in small towns
- Balancing professional responsibilities with family life

## A Bit About My Background

For those who don't know me, I wear many hats:
- **Deputy IT Director & CISO** at Latah County
- **President and Founder** of CommUnity NexUs, Inc. nonprofit
- **President and Founder** of the Do Something Amateur Radio Club (DSARC)
- **Unit Commander** for Latah County Auxiliary Communications
- **County Emergency Coordinator** for ARES
- **EMT/Firefighter** with Genesee Fire Department

I'm also a proud father to my 5-year-old son AJ and husband to my wife Heather. Family comes first, always.
